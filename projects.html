BPI Alternative Metrics Model for Loan Eligibility

Developed an AI-driven model using Isolation Forest algorithm to assess loan eligibility behavior for underserved MSMEs in the agricultural and fisheries sector. aggregated unconventional metrics such as bad loan count, customer ADB, and loan indicator which contributed onto building a cohesive pipeline for prediction. Awarded 3rd Place at BPI DataWave 2024 for its innovative approach to assessing loan eligibility.

DashGov

A governance dashboard that simplifies access to national budget data, empowering working-class citizens to understand how public funds are used and actively engage in governance decisions. DashGov simplifies complex government data, like the national budget, into clear, interactive charts and graphs, helping citizens understand public spending and engage in governance. Awarded Top 5 Finalist at the GDSC InnOlympics 2025 Hackathon.

EcoMarket

EcoMarket is an e-commerce platform dedicated to offering a range of eco-friendly, sustainable products that emphasize environmental responsibility. Additionally, by providing access to products that meet high standards for sustainability, EcoMarket seeks to educate consumers about the environmental impact of their purchases and encourage more eco-conscious shopping habits. The platform’s main objectives are threefold: to raise awareness among consumers, to minimize the environmental footprint of the shopping process, and to help EcoMarket establish a strong brand reputation as a pioneer in sustainable e-commerce.

Fake News Detection using LSTM

A Real and Fake News Classifier with Machine Learning. In this model, the text from news sources(input) are extracted, tokenized, analyzed by its usage, fed into the model as training data, and then used to determine whether the news is real or not based on the text it contains as its corresponding output.
